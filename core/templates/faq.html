Tutoring

How do I know if I need tutoring?

There’s no hard and fast rule as to who needs tutoring. If you feel that you could benefit from a little extra help and explanation, then tutoring might be for you!

I think I’ve just fallen a little bit behind, can you help catch me up?

Absolutely! As long as you know which topics you’ve covered I can re-teach them from scratch. It tends to go a lot faster than in class as I can go at your pace and spend time making sure you understand everything.

What do you do during a tutoring session?

Whatever you want really! I find the students that get the most out of my sessions are those who’ve done questions (eg from the textbook) at home, and bring in the ones they’re having trouble with. If you’ve missed a topic or completely forgotten one from earlier in the year I can also re-teach sections of the course.

Isn’t it only students who are failing that need tutoring?

Not at all! In the modern schooling system it’s hard for every student to get the attention they need from the teacher, so a private tutor makes sure that you understand all concepts properly and don’t fall behind. Also if you need to get certain scores to get into the course of your choice then I can help you achieve them.

Which subjects do you tutor?

I tutor Maths Methods, Specialist Maths, Further Maths, Physics and Chemistry. I also tutor maths and science at lower levels.

What levels do you tutor?

I specialize in VCE, however I also tutor IB and lower levels (Years 8-10). If you’re looking for university tutoring, please CONTACT ME directly as it depends on the subject and level.

I can’t make it to your place, do you offer online tutoring?

Yes I do. I can help you over email, or phone and a screen sharing program.

How long is a normal lesson?

I usually do lessons of 1 hour, but if you prefer 1.5 or 2 hours, then I’m happy to do that too.

How often do students normally have lessons?

Usually once a week, though I do have students who have lessons more often, so can do twice a week if you want it.

I probably only need help occasionally - do I have to commit to lessons every week?

Nope, not at all. I do have a waiting list of students who have occasional lessons when one of my other students cancels and a spot opens up. Feel free to contact me even if you’re only looking for a couple of lessons!

What references can you provide?

I have plenty of past students and parents who have told me they are happy to speak to any new potential students so feel free to ask me and I’ll get you in contact with them. I also have a valid Working With Children Check.

How much do you charge for private tutoring?

At the time of writing, my rate is $60 per hour, though you can contact me to double check this.

Which days do you tutor?

I tutor Mondays-Thursdays, usually after school, though if you happen to finish early one day I can do earlier. I don’t tutor weekends as I’m often away and it can be disruptive if we miss lessons.

Do you have any special discounts or promotions?

I offer a free trial lesson as I know a big part of choosing a tutor is finding the right match. This way you can have an obligation-free lesson to see if we get along.

Videos

What do you cover in your videos?

I have videos on pretty much the entire Methods course and I am in the process of making ones covering the Physics course.
The Methods videos are designed to give you a background explanation of the theory, followed by at least one concrete example of how to apply what you have just leaned.
The Physics videos all have a physical demonstration (or as close as is possible) of the concept that they explain. This allows you to see the physics in action and hopefully makes it a bit more interesting! The videos explain the concept along with the main formulas, but do not cover the derivations of these formulas (not required in VCE). Usually an example is incorporated within the experiment.
